Privacy & Cookies Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This policy explains how Bike Bratislava collects and uses your personal data and cookies, and the rights you have under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). It applies to bikebratislava.com.

1. Who we are (Controller)

Bike Bratislava is a guided cycling-tour service operated by Velocity (velocity.sk). The data controller can be reached at:

  • Mgr. Silvia Karais
  • Email: silvia@velocity.sk
  • Phone: +421 903 214 013
  • Bratislava, Slovakia

The operator's full official policies are also available here: Spracovanie osobných údajov and Používanie cookies.

2. What data we collect

  • Contact / inquiry form: your name, country of origin, email address, phone number (optional), travel dates, group size, experience level, preferred ride type, and your message.
  • Analytics (only with your consent): anonymous usage statistics via Google Analytics 4 — for example pages viewed, approximate region, and device/browser type.

We do not knowingly collect special-category (sensitive) data. Please do not include sensitive personal information in the message field.

3. Why we use it & legal basis

  • To answer your inquiry and prepare a quote or booking — legal basis: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) and/or steps taken at your request prior to a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • To measure and improve the website — legal basis: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a)), which you can withdraw at any time.

4. Cookies & similar technologies

We ask for your consent before setting any non-essential cookies. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time using the link (also in the footer).

Strictly necessary

A small record of your cookie choice, stored in your browser's localStorage under the key bb-cookie-consent. No tracking; always active.

Analytics (optional)

Google Analytics 4 sets cookies such as _ga and _ga_<id> to distinguish users and sessions. They load only after you accept analytics. IP addresses are anonymised and Google Consent Mode is applied.

5. Sharing & processors

We share data only with service providers acting on our behalf:

  • Google Ireland Ltd. — web analytics (Google Analytics 4), only if you consent.
  • Our email / hosting provider — to deliver and store your inquiry.

We never sell your personal data.

6. International transfers

Google may process data outside the European Economic Area. Such transfers rely on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses and the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

7. How long we keep it

Inquiry data is kept only as long as necessary to handle your request and for a reasonable follow-up period, after which it is deleted. Analytics data is retained according to the Google Analytics retention settings.

8. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction and portability of your data, to object to processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact silvia@velocity.sk.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Slovak supervisory authority — Úrad na ochranu osobných údajov Slovenskej republiky (dataprotection.gov.sk).

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version.
