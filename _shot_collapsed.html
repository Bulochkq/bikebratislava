Find Your Perfect Ride

Whether you're discovering Bratislava for the first time or searching for your next cycling challenge, we offer guided experiences for every type of rider.

Category 1

E-Bike & Leisure Tours

Designed for visitors who want to explore Bratislava and its surroundings in a relaxed and enjoyable way. These guided rides combine culture, history, local stories and beautiful scenery, making them perfect for travellers, couples, families and leisure riders.

Bratislava Highlights Tour

Discover the city's historic centre, the Danube waterfront and hidden local gems.

Danube Discovery Tour

Enjoy a scenic ride along one of Europe's most famous rivers.

Wine & Villages Tour

Explore charming wine towns, vineyards and local culture beyond the city.

Sunset Ride

Experience Bratislava during the most beautiful hours of the day.

Category 2

Road & Gravel Cycling Experiences

Created for passionate cyclists looking for longer distances, more demanding routes and unforgettable scenery. Ride through vineyards, rolling countryside, quiet roads and cross-border routes in the heart of Central Europe.

Cross Border Ride

Cycle between Slovakia and Austria on scenic roads and cycle paths.

Little Carpathians Road Ride

Rolling terrain, vineyard landscapes and some of the region's best cycling roads.

Gravel Through The Vineyards

A perfect combination of adventure, nature and local wine country.

Four Countries Challenge

Our signature endurance experience connecting multiple countries in a single ride.

Category 3

Trail & MTB Adventures

For riders looking for more technical terrain, forest trails and off-road adventures. Explore the Little Carpathians and discover some of the best trail riding opportunities around Bratislava.

Forest Trails Explorer

A fun introduction to Bratislava's trail network.

Little Carpathians MTB Ride

A guided mountain bike adventure through forests, ridgelines and scenic viewpoints.

Trail Discovery Experience

A mix of flowing singletracks, forest roads and local favourites.

Custom MTB Adventure

Tailor-made trail rides adapted to your skill level and riding style.
